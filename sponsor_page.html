The Motive

Why we love doing it.

At NP Hacks, we don't want to just host lessons or classes, we want to help develop strong, talented highschool coders who can take on any coding challenge. Our club for highschool students is a place where you can learn, grow, and innovate. We believe that by providing a platform for students to explore their passion for coding, we can help them discover their potential and prepare them for a future in technology.


This is why we need your help.

Coding Is Fun—But Not Alone

We are in the middle of a digital revolution. Technology is the fastest-growing industry in Canada, with hundreds of thousands of unfilled jobs and employers desperate for talent.

While tech companies are expanding rapidly in cities, education in technical literacy elsewhere has lagged significantly, and many of these classes fail to truly excite students about technology.

Students are talented and motivated, but the ability to explore technology locally is next to none. The only way to revitalize our city with technical literacy is to provide avenues to expose students to programming—starting with NP Hacks.

This is where your help comes in; to help fund NP Hacks.

Since day one, we have been committed to helping students fall in love with coding through fun and engaging experiences outside of lessons. With your support and assistance, we can make a big change together!
