ABOUT US

Here is what we do for you.

At NP Hacks, we aim to build the next generation of powerful, skilled, and talented coders who can solve problems, develop logical reasoning, and outperform others. Ran by students @ 10 N Park Dr Brampton, ON, NP Hacks believes that we can continue to bring together a community of promising and dedicated talent in the field of technology and inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts and give them the opportunity to find their passion in programming.

GET STARTED

Get your coding career starting & growing with NP Hacks.

Hackathons

Join our sponsored & club-held hackathons to collaborate, innovate, and create amazing projects with fellow developers and designers.

Workshops

Participate in hands-on workshops to enhance your skills and learn from industry experts and experienced mentors.

Mentorship

Get personalized guidance from experienced mentors to help accelerate your coding journey and career growth.

WHY WE STARTED

Our Consensus

Connect Coding & People

With how big the tech industry is, we aim to connect students with coding and make their journey easier and more accessible.

Connect With Other Coders

Connect with enthusiastic teenage coders who are passionate for programming and want to build projects

Build Portfolios

Coding alone can be isolating or troublesome. We bring together like-minded students who can support and inspire each other to create meaningful coding projects.

Mentor Students

We help get you personalized guidance from experienced mentors to help accelerate your coding journey and career growth.

Inspire Innovation

We empower students to think big, take risks, and build solutions that make a real difference.

Make An Impact

We want to build the next generation of impactful and useful coders. Using learned skills, we want to empower students to think big and use their knowledge to do good.

Gain Credible Experience

Gain valid, credible, and unique tech experience at our sponsored coding events to build your portfolio and experience. Sponsored by local & nation-wide tech-companies, we offer the BEST coding experience with valid brands and people!
